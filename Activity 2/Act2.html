ABOUT ME

Meet Allysa Jay Hipolito, affectionately known as Ali or Ally by friends and loved ones. Ali is not just your average individual; she is a passionate gamer and game developer, finding joy and creativity in the world of computer games. Beyond the digital realm, Ali shares a deep love for feline companions, particularly her cherished Siberian cat named Tabby. With a heart for both the virtual and the real, Ali's world is beautifully intertwined with the pixels of gaming and the soft purrs of her beloved feline friend.

"The Song of Achilles" holds a special place in my heart as my favorite book. It's a captivating exploration of Greek mythology, highlighting the profound connection between Achilles and Patroclus. Madeline Miller's eloquent prose brings these legendary figures to life, revealing the complexities of their friendship and love. Against the backdrop of the Trojan War, the novel skillfully navigates themes of passion and loyalty, offering a fresh and poignant perspective on timeless tales.
